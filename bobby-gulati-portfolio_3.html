About Me

Your Trusted Partner in Wealth, Protection & Legacy

I'm Bobby Gulati, an independent Financial Consultant based in Ontario, Canada. I specialize in helping incorporated business owners, entrepreneurs, high-income earners, and families build, protect, and transfer their wealth with intention.

Working across multiple carriers, I find the right solutions — not just the most convenient ones. Every plan I build is tailored to your unique situation, timeline, and legacy goals.

Financial education is the foundation of every great decision. My role isn't to sell products — it's to ensure you understand exactly what you're protected for, and what opportunities you may be missing.
